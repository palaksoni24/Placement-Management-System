Who are we?


Hello, Welcome to Placement Cell. Our company is based on helping students from different colleges get placed in top-rated IT companies like Infosys, Wipro, Tata Consultancy Services, Capgemini, Reliance Jio, Legato, Mphasis, etc. We provide opportunities for bright minds to get placed in top brand companies. The Placement Cell aims to raise the level of information support for students, offer wider industry exposure, encourage more students to take relevant training courses, and promote entrepreneurship within the student community.
